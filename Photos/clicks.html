These are some photos I took with my mobile camera. Some older photos were taken with a Nokia Lumia 625, which has a 5MP camera; the newer ones are from a Vivo Y73 with a 64MP (16MP) camera. You will be seeing the photos taken by the Lumia first. Photos in landscape mode are viewed best in full-screen mode I know what some of the objects in the images are, but to identify some insects, fungi, and plants, I rely on Google Lens.

The default delay for the slideshow is 2 seconds. Feel free to stop the slideshow and manually scroll through the images.

There are also a few images of insects and dead animals, so if you don’t like that, you know what to do.


If you have more information about any of the images shown, Or you want to use these in your work, I’d love to hear from you through the contact form.
